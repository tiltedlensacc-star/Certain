Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Certain. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Certain is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Data: All safety items, confirmations, and photos you create are stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to Certain Plus, Apple processes your payment through their In-App Purchase system. We receive only confirmation of your subscription status.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the app's functionality:

  • Store your safety items and confirmation records on your device
  • Verify your Certain Plus subscription status
  • Provide you with peace of mind through our confirmation tracking features

Data Storage and Security

All your personal data (items, confirmations, photos) is stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this information, and it is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Photos you attach to items are stored in your device's local storage and remain under your control.

Third-Party Services

We use Apple's In-App Purchase system for subscription management. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle your payment data.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete individual items at any time within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data from your device
  • We cannot recover deleted data as we do not store it on our servers

Children's Privacy

Certain is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally on the device, parents and guardians maintain control over their children's use of the app.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access all your data directly within the app
  • Modify or delete any items or confirmations
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
